Through the generosity of our donors, the Gujarati Muslim Association of America, is able to support various programs that benefit our schools and students. Please visit the Education and Scholarship program to see updates on these programs and learn how you..

GMAA has been working very effectively in those areas of Education, Financial Assistance, and Scholarships to needy and deserving students, teacher’s motivation program, widows and financially disadvantaged sisters program, for the last 30 years.

student Scholarships

The Gujarati Muslim Association of America, awards over 20 scholarships to Gujarati Muslim Students from Gujarat, India every year. These scholarships are made possible through generous donations from community members. Each of these scholarships honors past Alumni, current Alumni, or esteemed Gujarati Muslim community members from Chicago and surrounding cities with others.

HOW TO APPLY

The application process for these scholarships is conducted through Br. Farooq Bawani from Rajkot, Gujarat and applications are available each year after summer Break. Students can pick up applications from him or contact:

Br. Farooq Bawani: 98791 88179

GMAA INCLUDE

The Gujarati Muslim Association of America (GMAA) is non-profit 501 (C) (3) Charitable Organization, based in Lombard, IL, USA. The GMAA is working for Gujarati Muslim community in America and Gujarat for enlistment of community in the area of education and social status.
